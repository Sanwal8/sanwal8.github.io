Publications & Preprints
OpenSlideFM: Efficient Foundation Model for Computational Pathology
Manuscript in Preparation
A dual-scale transformer foundation model achieving 81.21% accuracy on pan-cancer classification with only 35M parameters. Pre-trained on 20,000 TCGA slides across 31 cancer types, deployable on consumer GPUs (RTX 4090, 24GB).
Sentinel-API: Staffable ICU Early-Warning System
Under Review at Scientific Reports (Nature)
Multi-horizon mortality prediction system with calibrated multi-center validation and alert-budget guardrails. Translates threshold choices into alerts per 100 admissions, enabling monitored roll-out ready for governance approval.
Guard-Constrained Human-Robot Collaboration for Warehouse Operations
Under Review at International Journal of Production Research
Digital twin framework for evaluating robot escort policies, calibrated against 8,535 production waves (MAPE ≈ 3%). Achieves −1.8% to −3.1% median ergonomic improvement with 48.9-62.2% adoption rate.
IHGAMP: Integrative Histopathology-Genomics Analysis for HRD Prediction
In Progress
Framework predicting homologous recombination deficiency directly from H&E whole-slide images. Validated across multiple CPTAC cancer cohorts including BRCA, CCRCC, and UCEC. Trained on 20,007 TCGA slides from 10,796 patients.
